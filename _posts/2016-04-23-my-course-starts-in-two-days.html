Well, that is seriously exciting!!

The last three months have been flying and I just cannot wait to get started with the pre-course at Makers. In a week I will be waving Environ goodbye and thanking them for all they have given and taught me in the last 5 years.

I am taking a break from coding this weekend and spend time with Lizzie (my girlfriend) as this is one out of two free weekends I get for the next three months. The rest of the time I will be learning (unless I am sleeping or eating!).

Anyway, this morning I published my first Kata on codewars! Codewars is a very addictive website where users can add coding challenges (called 'Katas') for others to solve. The challenges are ranked and the more you solve the more points you get. After a couple of months of just 'using' the service I am finally giving back.

Anyway, my challenge is currently in Beta status, waiting for the community to have a look and -hopefully- approve it. You can find it here.

I am not very happy with my own solution to my own challenge and I am not sure if there is a smarter and more elegant way to do it, which is a little embarrassing, but hopefully I will start getting feedback from the community soon and see where I stand.
